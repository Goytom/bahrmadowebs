Building the Bridge Between Turkish Markets and Ethiopian Businesses.

Our Story

Bahrmado was born from a simple observation: Ethiopian boutique owners and distributors are incredibly passionate about bringing quality products to their customers, but the challenges of sourcing from a vibrant, fast-paced market like Turkey are significant. The travel, the language barriers, the supplier negotiations, and the complex logistics can be overwhelming.

We wanted to close that gap. Based in the heart of Turkey, we founded Bahrmado to act as a dedicated, trusted partner for Ethiopian entrepreneurs. We are more than a service; we are your personal team on the ground.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Ethiopian businesses by providing seamless, end-to-end access to the vast opportunities within the Turkish market. We handle the complexities of sourcing and shipping so you can focus on what you do best: growing your business and delighting your customers.
