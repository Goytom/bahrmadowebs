Reliable Logistics from Turkey to Ethiopia

Fast, Efficient, and Transparent Shipping.

Getting your goods from Turkey to Ethiopia shouldn't be a mystery. Our logistics service is built on efficiency and clear communication. We ship everything from a single box of clothing to heavy machinery, ensuring a smooth process from pickup to delivery.

Choose the Right Shipping for Your Needs

Air Cargo: For Speed and Urgency

When you need your products quickly, air cargo is the ideal solution. Perfect for high-value fashion items and urgent orders. We manage everything from airport-drop-off to customs paperwork to ensure the fastest possible transit time.

Best for: Fashion, Electronics, Urgent Documents.

Sea Cargo: For Bulk and Cost-Efficiency

For larger, heavier, and less time-sensitive shipments, sea cargo offers the most cost-effective solution. We consolidate your goods and handle all port and customs formalities.

Best for: Building Materials, Machinery, Bulk Textiles.
