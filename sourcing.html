Expert Fashion Sourcing in Turkey

Your Personal Sourcing Team. We Find the Fashion, You Focus on Your Customers.

Finding the right clothing for your Ethiopian boutique can be challenging and expensive. Bahrmado removes the barriers. We act as your dedicated sourcing agents on the ground in Turkey, navigating the vast market to find products that match your brand's style, quality, and budget.

Our Sourcing Process: A Partnership for Success

Step 1: Understanding Your Vision

We start with a detailed consultation via WhatsApp or a call to learn about your boutique, your target audience, and the specific styles, materials, and price points you're looking for.

Step 2: Market & Supplier Research

Our team dives into the Turkish fashion market, from large manufacturers to unique local designers, to identify the best potential suppliers for your needs.

Step 3: Curated Selection & Virtual Showroom

We send you photos, videos, and detailed descriptions of the products. We handle all supplier communication and negotiate the best prices on your behalf.

Step 4: Quality Assurance

Before any purchase is finalized, we conduct thorough quality checks to ensure the products meet your standards. We are your quality guarantee.

Step 5: Secure Purchasing & Consolidation

Once you approve, we manage the secure purchase and consolidate all your items, preparing them for shipment.
